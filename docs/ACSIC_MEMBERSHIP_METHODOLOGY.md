# ACSIC Membership Methodology

## Current roster

Formal membership follows the official 2026 Global Symposium and 38th ACSIC member page published by CGTMSE. It lists 20 institutions across 14 countries or economies. ACGF is added only as an Observer based on Taiwan SMEG's current Member Institutions page.

The CGTMSE page has no stated publication date, so `publicationDate` is `null`; `sourceContextYear` is 2026 and the actual access date is 2026-07-16.

## Roster names versus official websites

Membership evidence and official-website evidence are separate. The current Taiwan SMEG roster contains two known outbound-link errors:

1. PHILGUARANTEE is linked to the Central Bank of Sri Lanka. The production record instead uses `https://www.philguarantee.gov.ph/`, verified from PHILGUARANTEE's official public site.
2. ACGF is linked to the Kyrgyz Republic Guarantee Fund. The production record instead uses `https://www.acgf.org.tw/`, verified from ACGF's official public site.

Tests reject both incorrect substitutions.

## Deprecated historical roster

`https://www.smeg.org.tw/en/basic/?node=31` is `deprecated_for_current_membership` and may be used only for `historical_reconciliation_only`. It is not present in production institution sources because it lists only 17 members, contains old names/domains, nests local Japanese CGCs under JFG, and omits ACGF's observer status.

Japan's 51 local Credit Guarantee Corporations are JFG constituents, not 51 additional ACSIC members. JFG remains one ACSIC record.

## Identity resolution

Aliases such as CGC Malaysia/CGCMB, Taiwan SMEG, SME Corporation, CGC PNG, OJSC Guarantee Fund and PT Askrindo are stored on their canonical record. Alias matching never creates another institution.
